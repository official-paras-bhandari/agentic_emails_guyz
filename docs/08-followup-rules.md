# Follow-up rules

Follow-ups are temporarily scheduled for steps 1-4 at 2 minutes after the original send. Every execution re-runs the central contact policy immediately before queueing.

No follow-up is allowed after a reply, unsubscribe, bounce, suppression, block, campaign stop, Gmail disconnect, daily limit, or completion of step 4. Completed sequences enter a 60-day cooldown; negative replies enter a 90-day cooldown.
